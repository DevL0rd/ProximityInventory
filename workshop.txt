description=[h1]Proximity Inventory[/h1]
Loot like a vacuum cleaner — one tab that scoops up every container around you.

[i]Like this? Also grab [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3740163599]Safehouse Inventory[/url] — and [url=https://github.com/DevL0rd/Zomboid-ProximityInventory]contribute / report bugs on GitHub[/url].[/i]

[b]Proximity Inventory[/b] adds a single tab to the loot window that merges every nearby container into one list: crates, shelves, corpses, car boots, the floor. Stop clicking through fifteen cupboards — grab everything from one place.

[h2]What it does[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]One tab, everything nearby.[/b] All the containers in reach, merged into a single inventory.
[*][b]Stays put.[/b] The tab keeps itself selected as you move around — it only switches when you click a different container.
[*][b]Loot without losing it.[/b] Grab items straight from the tab and stay right where you are.
[*][b]Snaps back when you wander off.[/b] Walk away from everything and it locks onto the proximity tab again, ready for the next pile.
[*][b]Spot the source.[/b] Optional highlighting of the containers a nearby item is in.
[*][b]Put it back where it came from.[/b] Drag an item onto the tab and it's returned to the exact container you grabbed it from.
[/list]

[h2]Why you'll like it[/h2]
Clearing a house, a gas station, or a fresh pile of zombies? Everything in arm's reach shows up in one tidy list, so you can scoop, sort, and bail faster.

[h2]Plays nice[/h2]
Works with vanilla and is friendly with CleanUI. Single-player and multiplayer both supported.
